Frequently Asked Questions

What condition do items need to be in?

We accept items in good, excellent, or like-new condition. Items should be clean, free of stains, tears, or excessive wear.

How long does the process take?

From shipping to getting paid typically takes 2-3 weeks. We'll notify you as soon as your items are listed for sale.

What if my items don't sell?

Items that don't sell within 90 days are donated to charity partners. You can also request items to be returned for a small fee.

How do I get paid?

You can choose to be paid via PayPal or check. Payments are processed monthly for items that have sold.
