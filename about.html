About ReWear

We're on a mission to make sustainable fashion accessible to everyone. By connecting buyers and sellers of pre-loved clothing, we're reducing waste and creating a circular economy.

Our Mission

Style Sustainably. Wear Again.

At ReWear, we believe that fashion should be both beautiful and sustainable. Our platform connects conscious consumers with quality pre-loved clothing, making it easy to buy, sell, and rent fashion items.

Every year, millions of tons of clothing end up in landfills. We're changing that by creating a marketplace where every item gets a second life. Whether you're looking to declutter your closet or find unique pieces, ReWear makes sustainable fashion choices effortless.

Our Story

How It All Began

ReWear was founded in 2020 by a group of friends who were frustrated by the wastefulness of fast fashion. We noticed that perfectly good clothing was being discarded while people were still looking for affordable, quality fashion.

What started as a small local marketplace has grown into a thriving community of fashion enthusiasts who believe in the power of second-hand clothing. Today, we're proud to serve customers across the country, helping them make sustainable fashion choices.

Our journey has been driven by a simple belief: that fashion can be both stylish and sustainable. We're excited to continue growing our community and making a positive impact on the planet.
