Rental FAQ

How long can I rent items?

Rental periods range from 1 day to 2 weeks. Longer rentals typically offer better daily rates.

What if I damage an item?

We understand accidents happen. Minor wear is expected, but significant damage may incur a repair fee.

Do I need to dry clean items?

No! We handle all cleaning. Simply return items as-is using the prepaid return label.

Can I extend my rental?

Yes, you can extend your rental up to 48 hours before the return date, subject to availability.

What sizes are available?

We offer sizes XS-XXL for most items. Check individual listings for specific size availability.

How does shipping work?

We offer free shipping on all rentals. Items arrive 1-2 business days before your rental start date.
